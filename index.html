About Weaving, Spinning and Dyeing

by Rachel Brown

In many primitive cultures weaving, spinning and dyeing constitute one of the major occupations, possibly second only to farming. In our technical society, because we are free from the need to pursue these crafts, the majority of us are not acquainted with the pleasure they give. Guild Activities

Natural Dye Kits

The kits include everything you need to get started using Natural Dyes. A small skein of yarn, ready to dye(mordanted), and natural plant materials. If you have any questions about the kits please email Sandy Voss, sandyvoss@cybermesa.com, or Glenna Dean, puchteca@windstream.net

Taos Wool Festival

Once again Art Through The Loom will have a booth at Taos Wool Festival in 2019. Be sure to stop by and get wonderful deals on handwoven rugs, handspun yarn, hand felted items and other handmade items. Last years booth was fantastic. Thank you to everyone who purchased our items.

August Santa Fe Show

In 2019 Art Through The Loom will have an indoor show in Santa Fe. Santa Fe is one of the top art cities in the United States, we expect this show to be very successful and a lot of fun for members and attendees.

Interested in learning more about weaving.

Consider joining Art Through The Loom. Our guild includes weavers with many levels of experience. The knowledge includes working with natural dyes and spinning yarn.
